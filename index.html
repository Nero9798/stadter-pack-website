About the Pack

The Stadter Pack is a modpack revolving around a casual, mundane goal: to build the most appealing base, whether it be a house, a town, or a city.

It contains various utilities to assist in this aspect, furthering the style, scale, and standard of Minecraft builds. Each mod was handpicked by myself to maintain a consistent style, primarily the low poly aesthetic, centered around mods like Yuushya Townscape and Cluttered.

"Each mod contains decorations made in this specific style to infuse themselves into vanilla and enhance the beauty of Minecraft builds."
